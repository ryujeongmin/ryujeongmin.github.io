Ballie

Ballie is Samsung’s AI-powered home companion robot. Designed as a small rolling assistant, it autonomously navigates your home, connects to smart devices, and provides personalized support through projection-based interfaces.

At CES 2024, Ballie showcased its ability to project interactive displays on walls, floors, and even ceilings — turning ordinary surfaces into contextual interfaces. It can follow you around, manage connected appliances, monitor your home environment, and even assist pets when you’re away.

Design & Development

As a member of Samsung’s VD Orbit Group, I contributed to Ballie’s mechanical design and prototyping, focusing on motion stability, modular hardware structure, and manufacturability. The project involved close collaboration between mechanical, electrical, and software teams to achieve a seamless physical-AI integration.
