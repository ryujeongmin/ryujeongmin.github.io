PROJECT

Ballie

Samsung's AI-powered home companion robot. Designed as a small rolling assistant, it autonomously navigates, connects to smart devices, and provides personalized support through projection-based interfaces.

At CES 2024, Ballie showcased its ability to project interactive displays on any surface, turning the home into a contextual interface.

Design & Development

As a member of Samsung's VD Orbit Group, I contributed to Ballie's mechanical design and prototyping. My focus was on motion stability, modular hardware structure, and manufacturability, ensuring a seamless integration of physical form and AI capabilities.

Official Demo Video

The official demonstration highlights Ballie's ability to autonomously navigate, project contextual displays, and act as a personalized home assistant. It showcases how the robot interacts with the environment and provides utility in daily life.
