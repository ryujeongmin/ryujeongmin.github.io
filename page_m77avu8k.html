Page Turner

For people who have difficulties in using hands, it automatically turns the page.
User can control the machine with the ultra sonic sensor. Depending on how close the user places their head, the machine takes specific actions.

Process

First, when the user puts their hand or head closer to the sensor (10cm or lower), the motors that hold the book open on both sides, allowing the book to be held. After fixing the book, if user places their hand or head 10 to 30cm away from the sensor, the right motor that fixes the page unfolds, the motor on the top turns slightly to lift the page slightly, and finally, the servo motor in the bottom of the middle turns the page.
