PROJECT

Umbrellatic

It features a mechanism that moves and opens an umbrella over the wheelchair space.
The umbrella should autonomously move, open, and close.
We also designed a desk with a weatherproof storage compartment suitable for her laptop.

Background

Vyoma Raman is a third-year UC Berkeley student studying CS and interdisciplinary studies! She doesn't have an effective and convenient way to keep herself and her laptop dry when it rains.

Weatherproofed Desk

The size of the first and second floors was made different by using double layers. It helps to prevent water leakage into the space inside the desk, and the size of the first layer was designed to be larger so that a sliding mechanism could be implemented without shaking from top and bottom.

Automatic umbrella opener Circuit

There are two switches.
One controls servo motors to release umbrella,
the other controls the stepper motors to pull the umbrella.
