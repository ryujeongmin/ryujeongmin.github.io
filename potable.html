Pot-able

Smart plant pot that senses and communicates its needs — living material meets ambient interaction.

TypeIoT · Product Design
RoleSolo Design & Build
Year2022
PlatformArduino
Overview

The plant speaks for itself

Many US consumers own at least one foliage plant, and most try to keep theirs as healthy as possible. Pot-able aims to promote the plant growth process by creating pots that communicate their own state. The LCD displays soil humidity and temperature; LED indicators change color when the plant needs attention.
Pot-able — smart plant communication device
Features

Sensing + display + transplant

LCD panel shows real-time soil humidity and temperature. Dual LEDs support photosynthesis and turn red/blue when humidity leaves the optimal range — red for too high, blue for too low. The porting design also guides the seedling transplanting process, which is critical for plant lifespan.
Prototyping

From CAD to working prototype

The project went through modeling, two prototype versions, and iterative testing. Each version refined the sensor integration and the ergonomics of the porting compartment. The final version successfully maintains stable sensor readings through the transplanting process without disturbing the root system.
