PROJECT

Image Perfume

It converts an image into a special perfume. By analyzing the RGB composition of a photo, the system creates a unique scent profile layered to match the visual memory.

Method

Extract RGB values from the picture. Each R, G, B channel corresponds to a specific base scent. The machine layers these scents according to the ratio of RGB values found in the image.

Sketch

Initial concept sketches exploring the form factor and interaction flow. The design focuses on a minimal, lab-like aesthetic that emphasizes the "extraction" process.

Process

1. Place the picture in the designated slot.
2. Press the button and wait for the machine to analyze the image and dispense the scent.
3. Enjoy the picture accompanied by its unique, specialized perfume.

News Coverage

Featured in HelloDD News. The project explores synesthetic experiences by bridging visual and olfactory senses through technology.
